Why GeoCam?

You draw a zone around your home in the app. When you leave and cross that zone, Blink arms. When you come back and re-enter, Blink disarms. One setup, then it runs in the background—no more "did I turn the cameras on?"

Home is always protected

Cameras arm as you drive away and disarm when you pull into the driveway. Your home is covered without you having to remember.

Zero daily effort

Stop opening the Blink app every time you leave or get home. GeoCam handles it so you can focus on your day.

Flexible for families

Adjust the zone size and sensitivity to fit your routine. With Pro, add family members so Blink arms only when everyone has left—perfect for households with different schedules.

How it works

You choose the area around your home (the "geofence"). When your phone leaves that area, GeoCam tells Blink to arm. When you come back inside the zone, it disarms. You can tweak the zone size and, with Pro, add other family phones so the system knows when everyone's away. It's built only for Blink—no extra hubs or hardware.
