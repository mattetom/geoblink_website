Why Use This App

Geofencing is an innovative technology that can be used to make home security more efficient and effective. With geofencing, you can set up virtual geographic areas, or "geofences," around your home. When you leave home and cross the geofence boundary, your Blink smart security system is automatically activated. Conversely, when you return home and cross the geofence, the Blink smart security is deactivated. This way, you no longer have to worry about manually activating or deactivating your Blink smart security.
